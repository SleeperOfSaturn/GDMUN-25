Echoes of Concord

What is
GDMUN?

At this point, do we even need to ask? Some of us have been here before, arguing, negotiating, surviving on caffeine and last-minute research. It has been four years since we dusted off our placards post lockdown and somehow, each edition has only gotten crazier (in the best way possible). The excitement never really faded and now, we’re here again, back to where it all begins. The brainstorming, the elaborate discussions, the never-ending checklists, all leading up to yet another edition—“GDMUN 2025.” Will it be bigger? Probably. Bolder? Most definitely. More fun? Without a doubt.

We take great pride in presenting the 5th edition of GDMUN, the 4th since the lockdown. As one of the most anticipated conferences in the circuit, GDMUN continues to set a high standard for debate and diplomacy.With every passing year, we aim to improve and set new benchmarks. The previous edition was a tremendous success with a large number of delegates participating from some of the most prestigious institutions. This year, we bring you seven committees providing delegates with the opportunity to engage in diverse agendas and explore the many facets of MUN. The Secretariat extends its best wishes to all participants and looks forward to welcoming you to yet another edition of GDMUN.
